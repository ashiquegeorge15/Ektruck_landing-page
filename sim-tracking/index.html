How Our Solution Works

Cellular network-based tracking without GPS hardware

1

SIM Activation

Standard SIM cards in mobile devices or IoT modules are registered in our tracking platform.

2

Network Triangulation

Cellular towers detect the SIM signal and triangulate its position using network data.

3

Data Processing

Location data is processed in our cloud platform, calculating position, speed, and route information.

4

Real-Time Monitoring

Users track shipments via web or mobile app with continuous updates and automated alerts.

5

Analytics & Reporting

System generates insights on delivery performance, route efficiency, and optimization opportunities.
