Key Features of Control Tower

Comprehensive tools to manage your fleet operations efficiently

Real-Time Fleet Monitoring & Tracking

With our real-time tracking system, fleet managers can monitor their vehicles at any moment, ensuring instant visibility and control. Whether it's SIM-based tracking or GPS-enabled tracking, our solution provides accurate location data, helping businesses improve fleet efficiency, reduce delays, and enhance customer satisfaction.

99.8% Tracking Accuracy

Automated Trip Creation & Closure

Gone are the days of manual trip tracking! Ektruck's Control Tower automatically creates trips, tracks progress, and auto-closes completed trips, eliminating paperwork and human errors. This feature not only saves time but also ensures better operational accuracy.

85% Reduction in Manual Work

Intelligent Expense Management

Managing fleet expenses is challenging, but our expense tracking system allows logistics operators to record, monitor, and analyze all operational costs. From fuel consumption and tolls to driver expenses, businesses can track every cost in real time and optimize their budgets for maximum profitability.

30% Cost Optimization

Advanced TAT & ETA Monitoring

Meeting delivery deadlines is critical in logistics. Our Turnaround Time (TAT) and Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) monitoring tools allow businesses to define expected timelines for deliveries and track performance. The system also alerts managers in case of delays, helping them take proactive measures to avoid disruptions.

95% On-time Delivery Rate

Seamless API & ERP Integrations

To ensure smooth data flow, our Managed Control Tower is equipped with EDI/API integrations that connect with Order Management Systems (OMS), Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions, and other logistics platforms. This seamless connectivity eliminates data silos, enhancing the efficiency of logistics operations.

50+ Integration Options

Instant Alerts & Notifications

Our system provides automated alerts and notifications for key events such as delays, route deviations, vehicle breakdowns, and fuel consumption anomalies. This enables managers to act quickly and ensure uninterrupted logistics operations.

Real-time Alert System

Centralized Control & Decision-Making

With Ektruck's Managed Control Tower, businesses gain a centralized hub for decision-making. Whether you are handling multi-location operations or managing a fleet of thousands of vehicles, our platform ensures seamless communication and coordination across all stakeholders.

100% Operational Visibility
