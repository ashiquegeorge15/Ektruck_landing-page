Elevating Mining & Heavy Equipment Operations

In the challenging world of mining and heavy equipment, success hinges on maximizing uptime, ensuring safety, and optimizing resources. ek(one)'s industry-hardened solutions are specifically engineered to withstand harsh mining environments while delivering actionable insights that drive productivity, enhance safety, and increase profitability.

1. Managed Control Tower for Mining Operations

Our Managed Control Tower serves as the nerve center for your mining operations, providing comprehensive visibility and control across your entire site—from extraction to processing to transportation.

Centralized Asset Visibility

Monitor your entire fleet of heavy equipment, haul trucks, generators, and mobile assets on a single dashboard with real-time updates, even in remote locations with limited connectivity.

Performance Analytics

Gain actionable insights into equipment performance, operator efficiency, and production metrics with powerful analytical tools and customizable reports.

Proactive Alert Management

Receive instant notifications for critical events, maintenance needs, safety concerns, and operational disruptions before they impact production.

2. ADAS/DMS Solutions for Mining Safety

In the high-risk mining environment, our Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) and Driver Monitoring Systems (DMS) significantly enhance operational safety and prevent costly accidents.

Collision Avoidance Technology

Ruggedized radar and camera systems detect potential collisions between heavy equipment, light vehicles, and personnel, triggering automated alerts and interventions.

Operator Fatigue Detection

AI-powered cameras monitor operator alertness in real-time, detecting early signs of fatigue, distraction, or impairment to prevent accidents before they happen.

Blind Spot Monitoring

Eliminate dangerous blind spots on large mining equipment with advanced camera systems and proximity sensors, providing operators with complete situational awareness.

Safety Impact

Our mining clients have reported:

  • 65% reduction in equipment-related incidents
  • 80% decrease in near-miss events
  • Full compliance with DGMS safety guidelines

3. Transport Management System for Mining Logistics

Our specialized Transport Management System (TMS) optimizes the movement of materials, equipment, and personnel across mining operations, enhancing efficiency and reducing costs.

Mining-Specific Route Optimization

AI-powered algorithms factor in haul road conditions, gradients, weather impacts, and load weights to determine optimal routes for maximum efficiency and minimal wear.

Load Management & Optimization

Ensure optimal loading of haul trucks and transport vehicles to maximize capacity utilization while preventing overloading and unnecessary equipment strain.

Compliance Documentation

Digitize and automate all transportation documentation, including load sheets, inspection records, and regulatory compliance reports, eliminating paperwork and errors.

Operational Impact

Average mining operation with 20 haul trucks:

Transport cycle time reduction: 15-20%

Increase in material moved per shift: 25-30%

Annual operational savings: ₹1.5-2 crore

4. Advanced Fuel Management for Mining

With fuel representing up to 40% of operational costs in mining, our comprehensive fuel management solution delivers significant savings through precision monitoring, theft prevention, and consumption optimization.

Real-time Consumption Monitoring

High-precision sensors and telemetry systems track fuel levels and consumption patterns across your mining fleet, even in extreme operating conditions.

Theft Prevention & Detection

Advanced algorithms detect unusual fuel level changes, unauthorized refueling events, and suspicious consumption patterns, with immediate alerts to security personnel.

Operator Efficiency Scoring

Analyze operator behavior and its impact on fuel consumption, identifying opportunities for training and operational improvements that reduce fuel waste.

"After implementing ek(one)'s fuel management solution, we reduced our monthly fuel expenses by 27%. The system paid for itself within three months."

— Operations Manager, Leading Iron Ore Mine

5. Intelligent Cargo Solutions for Mining

Secure and optimize the movement of valuable minerals, equipment, and supplies with our intelligent cargo management system designed for the unique challenges of mining operations.

Mineral Tracking & Security

End-to-end tracking of extracted minerals from pit to processing to market, with tamper-evident seals and real-time security monitoring for high-value cargo.

Load Weight Verification

Automated weight sensors ensure accurate measurement of extracted materials, preventing revenue loss from underreporting and ensuring compliance with transport regulations.

Chain of Custody Documentation

Maintain complete digital records of all material movements with blockchain-secured documentation that ensures regulatory compliance and simplifies audits.

6. SIM/Mobile-Based Visibility for Remote Operations

Our cost-effective SIM/mobile-based tracking solution provides essential visibility for smaller assets and personnel in remote mining operations, with minimal infrastructure requirements.

Low-Cost Asset Visibility

Track light vehicles, portable equipment, and personnel using standard mobile devices and ruggedized SIM-based trackers, without expensive hardware installations.

Multi-Network Connectivity

Seamless switching between cellular, satellite, and local mesh networks ensures continuous tracking even in areas with inconsistent connectivity.

Personnel Safety Monitoring

Emergency SOS buttons, man-down detection, and geo-fencing capabilities enhance personnel safety in hazardous mining environments.
