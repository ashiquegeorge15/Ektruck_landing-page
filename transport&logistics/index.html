1. Managed Control Tower

A managed control tower serves as the nerve center of your logistics operations, providing real-time visibility and control across your entire supply chain.

Real-time Fleet Monitoring

Track your entire fleet's location, status, and performance metrics in real-time with advanced telematics integration.

Predictive Analytics

Anticipate potential delays and disruptions before they impact your operations with AI-powered prediction models.

Centralized Command Center

Manage all aspects of your logistics operations from a single dashboard with customizable views and alerts.

Industry Insight

According to recent industry research, companies implementing control tower solutions experience a 37% reduction in supply chain disruptions and a 42% improvement in on-time deliveries.

2. Transport Management System (TMS)

Our Transport Management System (TMS) is a comprehensive platform that digitalizes and automates your entire transport operations. From order management to delivery confirmation, TMS provides end-to-end visibility and control.

Intelligent Route Optimization

AI-powered algorithms consider multiple factors including traffic, weather, and vehicle capacity to suggest optimal routes, reducing fuel consumption by up to 25%.

Automated Load Planning

Smart algorithms match loads with available vehicles, optimizing capacity utilization and reducing empty miles by 40%.

Digital Documentation

Eliminate paperwork with digital PODs, invoices, and compliance documents, reducing processing time by 70% and minimizing errors.

3. ADAS/DMS Solutions

Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) and Driver Monitoring Systems (DMS) represent the cutting edge in transport safety technology, significantly reducing accident rates and improving driver behavior.

Collision Prevention

Advanced sensors and AI algorithms detect and alert drivers to potential hazards, including forward collision, lane departure, and blind spot warnings.

Driver Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of driver alertness, distraction, and fatigue levels, with immediate alerts for dangerous behaviors like texting or drowsiness.

Performance Analytics

Comprehensive driver scorecards and performance metrics to identify training needs and recognize safe driving practices.

"Implementing ADAS/DMS technology reduced our accident rate by 63% in the first year alone, saving lives and over ₹1.2 crore in damages and insurance costs."

- Fleet Safety Manager, Leading Logistics Company

4. Fuel Management Solutions

Our comprehensive fuel management solution helps you monitor, control, and optimize fuel consumption across your fleet, addressing one of the largest operational expenses in transport.

Real-Time Fuel Monitoring

Track fuel levels, consumption patterns, and refueling events in real-time with advanced sensors integrated directly with vehicle systems.

Theft Prevention

Advanced algorithms detect and alert suspicious fuel drain events and consumption patterns, with immediate notifications to management.

Eco-Driving Insights

Identify and coach drivers on fuel-efficient driving techniques through detailed analysis of acceleration, braking, and idling patterns.

Did you know? Fuel typically represents 30-40% of total fleet operating costs. Our clients report average fuel savings of 15-20% after implementing our fuel management solution.

5. Temperature Monitoring Solutions

For temperature-sensitive cargo, our advanced monitoring system provides real-time visibility and alerts, ensuring product quality throughout the journey and reducing spoilage losses.

Multi-Zone Monitoring

Monitor multiple temperature zones within a single vehicle, with customizable thresholds for different product requirements and storage conditions.

Real-Time Alerts

Instant notifications for temperature deviations, door openings, and equipment malfunctions, allowing for immediate corrective action.

Compliance Documentation

Automated generation of temperature history reports for regulatory compliance and customer verification requirements.

Regulatory Compliance

Our system ensures compliance with:

  • GDP (Good Distribution Practice)
  • FSMA (Food Safety Modernization Act)
  • WHO Guidelines for storage and transport
  • FSSAI temperature requirements

6. Intelligent Cargo Management

Ensure complete visibility and security of your cargo with our intelligent tracking and monitoring solution, reducing theft, damage, and delivery exceptions.

Smart Lock System

Electronic locks with real-time tampering alerts and access logging, providing enhanced security for high-value shipments.

Environmental Monitoring

Track temperature, humidity, shock, and tilt conditions throughout the journey to ensure cargo integrity and reduce claims.

Asset Tracking

End-to-end visibility of individual packages and pallets with barcode/RFID integration for complete chain of custody documentation.

7. SIM/Mobile-Based Visibility Solutions

Our SIM-based tracking solution provides reliable visibility even in areas with limited GPS coverage or challenging connectivity environments.

Network-Agnostic Tracking

Leverages cellular networks for positioning when GPS signals are unavailable, ensuring continuous tracking in remote areas.

Low Power Consumption

Optimized power management extends battery life for long-haul journeys and reduces maintenance requirements.

Driver Mobile App

Companion app for drivers simplifies communication, document capture, and status updates without requiring specialized hardware.
