Manufacturers & Traders

For manufacturers and traders, efficient logistics and fleet management are key to reducing costs, improving delivery times, and scaling operations. Whether you run a manufacturing unit, distribution business, retail supply chain, or service-based fleet, EkTruck provides affordable, IoT-driven solutions to streamline your transport and logistics operations.

Challenges in Fleet & Market Truck Management

Key operational hurdles that manufacturers and traders face in logistics management

Lack of Real-Time Visibility

Delays and inefficiencies due to untracked shipments.

Inefficient Fleet Utilization

Poor truck allocation leads to increased idle time and costs.

High Fuel & Logistics Costs

Unmonitored fuel usage and detours increase operational expenses.

Unreliable Market Trucks

Difficulty in finding and managing verified third-party trucks.

Disconnected Systems

Lack of integration with ERP platforms leads to manual data entry and errors.
