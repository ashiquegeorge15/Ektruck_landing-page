Key Features of ADAS/DMS Solutions

Comprehensive safety features powered by AI technology

Driver Behavior Monitoring

Our AI-driven driver monitoring system (DMS) continuously tracks driver behavior, identifying signs of fatigue, distraction, drowsiness, and reckless driving. Instant alerts notify fleet managers, allowing timely intervention to prevent potential accidents.

24/7 Real-time Monitoring

Forward Collision Warning (FCW)

This feature detects potential collisions by analyzing road conditions and vehicle speed. If a potential accident is identified, the system issues real-time audio-visual alerts, allowing the driver to take immediate corrective action.

3-5s Advance Warning

Lane Departure Warning (LDW)

Unintentional lane changes can lead to dangerous accidents, especially on highways. Our lane departure warning system alerts drivers when they drift out of their lane without signaling, ensuring safer driving practices.

95% Detection Accuracy

Blind Spot Monitoring (BSM)

Blind spots are a major cause of trucking accidents. The blind spot monitoring system uses sensors and cameras to detect vehicles or obstacles in the driver's blind spots, preventing risky lane changes.

360Â° Vehicle Coverage

Seatbelt & Distraction Detection

Unfastened seatbelts and distracted driving are leading causes of road fatalities. Our system ensures compliance by detecting seatbelt usage and alerting drivers and fleet managers when unsafe behaviors, like mobile phone usage or smoking, are detected.

98% Detection Rate

Theft & Unauthorized Access Prevention

The ADAS/DMS Solutions integrate with video telematics to detect unauthorized access to the vehicle. Alerts are sent instantly, preventing theft and ensuring vehicle security.
