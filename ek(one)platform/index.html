User Rating Management

Two-Way Rating System: Building Trust & Transparency

At EkTruck, we believe in fostering a fair and transparent logistics ecosystem. Our Two-Way Rating System allows both truck owners and shippers to rate each other based on their experience, ensuring accountability and improved service quality.

How It Works

Mutual Rating & Feedback

After each completed trip, both the truck owner and the shipper can rate each other on key parameters such as reliability, communication, and professionalism.

Verified Reviews

Only users who have successfully completed a transaction can leave ratings, ensuring authenticity and credibility.

Continuous Improvement

Higher ratings help users build a strong reputation, while constructive feedback allows for service enhancements.

Benefits of Two-Way Rating

For Truck Owners

Work with reliable shippers who offer fair payments and clear communication.

For Shippers

Partner with trustworthy truck owners who deliver on time and maintain vehicle standards.

For the Industry

A transparent rating system encourages professionalism, safety, and efficiency.

At EkTruck, we empower our users to make informed decisions, ensuring trust, accountability, and better business relationships.

Real-Time Market Indices

Driving Transparency & Efficiency

In the highly dynamic spot trucking market, freight rates fluctuate based on demand, supply, fuel prices, and seasonal trends. Real-time market indices for market trucks provide data-driven freight pricing, ensuring transparency, fairness, and efficiency for all stakeholders, including small truck owners, brokers, and shippers.

How Real-Time Indices Work

AI-Powered Rate Benchmarking

Uses live market data, historical trends, and predictive analytics to determine fair pricing.

Demand-Supply Tracking

Monitors truck availability and freight demand across different regions to reflect accurate pricing.

Cost Component Analysis

Factors in fuel price fluctuations, toll charges, and operational expenses for real-time rate adjustments.

Lane-Wise Rate Indexing

Provides region-specific and route-specific freight indices, preventing price manipulation.

How Real-Time Market Indices Create Transparency

Eliminates Rate Manipulation

Data-driven pricing prevents overcharging or under-pricing by brokers or middlemen.

Empowers Small Truck Owners

Gives truckers real-time insights into competitive rates, ensuring fair earnings.

Enhances Shipper Confidence

Provides verified, market-driven rates, reducing uncertainties in freight costs.

Faster & Smarter Decision-Making

Instant rate visibility helps in quick load-booking and optimized fleet allocation.

Regulatory Compliance & Standardization

Helps in creating fair industry benchmarks, benefiting all stakeholders.

By leveraging real-time market indices, digital trucking platforms like ek(one) are transforming the spot trucking ecosystem, ensuring fair pricing, better negotiations, and enhanced operational efficiency for everyone.
