User Rating Management

Two-Way Rating System: Building Trust & Transparency

At EkTruck, we believe in fostering a fair and transparent logistics ecosystem. Our Two-Way Rating System allows both truck owners and shippers to rate each other based on their experience, ensuring accountability and improved service quality.

How It Works

Mutual Rating & Feedback

After each completed trip, both the truck owner and the shipper can rate each other on key parameters such as reliability, communication, and professionalism.

Verified Reviews

Only users who have successfully completed a transaction can leave ratings, ensuring authenticity and credibility.

Continuous Improvement

Higher ratings help users build a strong reputation, while constructive feedback allows for service enhancements.

Benefits of Two-Way Rating

For Truck Owners

Work with reliable shippers who offer fair payments and clear communication.

For Shippers

Partner with trustworthy truck owners who deliver on time and maintain vehicle standards.

For the Industry

A transparent rating system encourages professionalism, safety, and efficiency.

At EkTruck, we empower our users to make informed decisions, ensuring trust, accountability, and better business relationships.
