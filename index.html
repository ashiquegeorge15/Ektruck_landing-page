India's 1st Unified Trucking Platform

ektruck is designed to streamline logistics operations by seamlessly integrating with all major ERP systems and industry tools. Whether it's SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, or custom-built enterprise solutions, ektruck ensures real-time data flow for efficient order management, inventory tracking, freight optimization, and financial reconciliation.

By bridging the gap between logistics and enterprise systems, ektruck enhances visibility, automates workflows, reduces manual interventions, and enables data-driven decision-making. Its API-driven architecture allows effortless plug-and-play connectivity, ensuring businesses can leverage their existing infrastructure while maximizing efficiency and control over their trucking operations.
