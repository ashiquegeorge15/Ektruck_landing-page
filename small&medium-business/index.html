Small & Medium Business Solutions

For small and medium businesses (SMBs), efficient logistics and fleet management are key to reducing costs, improving delivery times, and scaling operations. Whether you run a manufacturing unit, distribution business, retail supply chain, or service-based fleet, EkTruck provides affordable, IoT-driven solutions to streamline your transport and logistics operations.

Challenges SMBs Face in Fleet & Logistics Management

Key operational hurdles that impact efficiency and profitability for growing businesses

High Transportation Costs

Fuel inefficiency, route mismanagement, and vehicle downtime impact profits.

Delayed Deliveries

Lack of real-time tracking leads to unreliable delivery schedules.

Limited Visibility

Manual fleet management makes tracking and optimization difficult.

Safety & Compliance

Unmonitored driver behavior and vehicle health lead to risks.

Resource Utilization

Underused or idle vehicles increase operational costs.
