LAST-MILE Oil & Gas Distribution

The oil and gas industry requires precision, safety, and efficiency in last-mile distribution to ensure timely and secure deliveries of fuel, LPG, and other petroleum products. EkTruck provides IoT-powered fleet solutions to enhance tracking, optimize delivery routes, ensure regulatory compliance, and minimize risks in fuel transportation.

Challenges in Oil & Gas Last-Mile Distribution

Key operational hurdles that impact efficiency and safety in fuel transportation

Delivery Delays

Traffic congestion and inefficient route planning impact fuel supply chains.

High Operational Costs

Unoptimized routes and excessive idling increase expenses.

Limited Visibility

Lack of real-time tracking can lead to mismanagement and pilferage.

Safety & Compliance

Transporting hazardous materials requires strict adherence to safety protocols.

Fleet Utilization

Underutilized vehicles lead to increased costs and inefficiencies.
