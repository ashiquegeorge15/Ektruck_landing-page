  1. Real-Time Truck Visibility: Live tracking of inbound and outbound market trucks, ensuring optimized scheduling and allocation.
  2. Automated Slot Management: Digital allocation of trucks reduced waiting time and improved dispatch efficiency at factories.
  3. Data-Driven Decision Making: AI-powered insights helped in route optimization, fleet utilization, and demand forecasting.
  4. Seamless Transporter Coordination: A centralized platform enabled faster communication and reduced delays between all stakeholders.
  5. Exception Management & Alerts: Instant notifications for delays, congestion, and compliance issues, allowing proactive resolution.

SAP ERP Integration

A key aspect of the solution was the seamless integration with the company's SAP ERP system:

  • Automated Order Processing: Orders created in SAP ERP were instantly reflected in the ektruck Control Tower, enabling seamless truck allocation.
  • Real-Time Load Planning: The system matched available market trucks with pending orders, optimizing fleet utilization.
  • Live Tracking & Status Updates: SAP ERP received live updates on truck movement, estimated arrival times, and delays, ensuring full transparency.
  • Exception Handling & Alerts: Any order fulfillment issues, truck delays, or load mismatches triggered instant alerts for proactive resolution.
  • Digitized Proof of Delivery (POD): Once goods were delivered, POD data synced back to SAP ERP, automating invoicing and settlement.
