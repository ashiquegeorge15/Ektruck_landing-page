How ektruck Digitalized End-to-End Logistics for a 3PL Partnering with a Leading Car Manufacturer

Problem Statement

A 3PL logistics provider responsible for transporting vehicles for a leading car manufacturer faced significant operational challenges due to a lack of digital systems for managing the end-to-end supply chain process. The key issues included:

  • No Real-Time In-Transit Monitoring – Fleet movement was tracked manually, leading to delays and lack of visibility.
  • Inefficient Unloading & POD Management – Manual proof-of-delivery (POD) processes caused delays in verification and invoicing.
  • Billing & ERP Integration Issues – The SAP ERP system of the car manufacturer only billed once VINs were delivered, causing payment delays.

Solution: ektruck's End-to-End Digital Logistics Platform

  • Digital Loading & Dispatch Management – Automated truck allocation & loading process reduced human errors and optimized vehicle placement.
  • Real-Time In-Transit Monitoring – Live GPS tracking & geofencing enabled 24/7 visibility of vehicle movements.
  • AI-Powered Route Optimization – Smart routing minimized transit delays and reduced fuel costs.
  • Seamless Unloading & Digital POD Management – Drivers uploaded e-PODs instantly, enabling real-time delivery confirmation.
  • Automated Billing & ERP Integration – Billing triggered instantly in SAP ERP once POD was verified, ensuring faster payments for the 3PL.
  • Exception Alerts & Predictive Insights – Automated alerts for delays, route deviations, and compliance issues helped in proactive issue resolution.

How ektruck Improved Driver Behavior for Last-Mile Deliveries in an Oil & Gas Company

Problem Statement

A leading Oil & Gas company faced significant driver behavior-related challenges in their last-mile fuel deliveries, impacting safety, efficiency, and compliance. The key issues included:

  • Unsafe Driving Practices – Speeding, harsh braking, sharp turns, and sudden accelerations led to safety risks and higher accident rates.
  • Delivery Delays & Inefficiencies – Unmonitored driver behavior resulted in route deviations, unnecessary stoppages, and delayed deliveries.
  • Excessive Fuel Consumption – Poor driving habits increased fuel wastage, leading to higher operational costs.
  • Lack of Real-Time Monitoring – No centralized system to track driver performance, violations, or compliance adherence.
  • Regulatory & Safety Non-Compliance – The company needed to meet strict oil & gas transportation safety standards, but manual supervision was ineffective.

Solution: ektruck's AI-Powered Driver Behaviour Monitoring System

To address these issues, the company implemented ektruck's AI-driven Driver Monitoring & Last-Mile Delivery Solution, providing real-time visibility, safety tracking, and automated performance analytics.

How ektruck Solved the Problem:

  • AI-Based Driver Behavior Analytics – Continuous monitoring of speed, braking, cornering, and acceleration patterns to identify risky behaviors.
  • In-Cab Alerts & Notifications – Real-time audio and visual alerts warned drivers about unsafe actions like speeding, fatigue, and distractions.
  • Route Optimization & Geo-Fencing – Automated trip planning ensured efficient last-mile routes, eliminating unauthorized stoppages and diversions.
  • Fuel Consumption & Idling Monitoring – AI-driven insights reduced fuel wastage by optimizing driving patterns.
  • Seamless Integration with Control Tower – Driver data was automatically analyzed and integrated with fleet operations for real-time insights.
  • Score-Based Driver Performance Reports – Drivers were ranked on safety and efficiency metrics, enabling targeted training and performance incentives.
  • Compliance & Safety Standardization – Digital tracking ensured adherence to Oil & Gas regulatory standards for safe transportation.
